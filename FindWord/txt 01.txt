Alexis Lingad is an ethical hacker and an entrepreneur. He is currently
the founder and CEO of Cryptors Cybersecurity Inc. He became the cyber
security consultant in iEi Securities USA. He became the Philippine Hacker
Games champion for 2015 beating the other PhD holder hackers and became
the champion again for Philippine Hacker Games 2017 where he beats the man
who hacked the Commission on Elections in the Philippines. Throughout his
journey, Alexis tour around the Philippines to raise cyber security awareness
fulfilling his vision to build a future where every person online will be aware
on how to secure themselves from hackers.
